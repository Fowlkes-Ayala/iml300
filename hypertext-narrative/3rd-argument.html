THE QUESTION OF THESEUS’ SHIP

Premise 1. Substituting a small artificial component into an entity with rights, if that component contributes identically to the entity’s psychology and does not affect relevant social relationships, does not affect that entity’s rights.

Premise 2.The process described in Premise 1 could possibly be iterated in a way that transforms a natural human being with rights into a wholly artificial being with the same rights.

Conclusion. Therefore, it is possible to create an artificial being with the same rights as those of a natural human being.
