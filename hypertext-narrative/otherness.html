The Objection of Otherness

Some hold a hierarchy of moral status that place AIs outside of our social contracts and circles of concern

Accordingly, we owe the greatest obligation to
family,
less to neighbors,
less to fellow citizens,
even less to distant foreigners,
and maybe nothing at all to those outside our species.


In this state of nature, every person (“us”) is enemy to every other person (“them”) until some social contract binds them together. Is this the world in which you want to live?
